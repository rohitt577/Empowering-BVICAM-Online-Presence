Activities At Bvicam

Workshops and Seminars not only present the students an insight of the industry, but also provide with the necessary inputs to enable them to churn out professionals. Activities like Faculty Development Programmes (FDPs) and Management Development Programmes (MDPs) are a regular feature at BVICAM. The Institute has conducted many FDPs and MDPs on the areas like SEI-CMM, ISO 9000, Networking, Embedded Systems, Information Security, etc. in which faculty members from Institutions across the country have participated, including the corporate delegates from reputed business houses. Additionally, Workshops, Seminars and Invited Talks are regularly being organized exclusively for students to enhance both Technical as well as Soft Skills. This also helps in strengthening the Industry-Institute relationship.
