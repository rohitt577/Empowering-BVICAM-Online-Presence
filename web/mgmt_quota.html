Admissions under 10% Management Category

It may further be noted that 90% of seats, of this Institute, are filled through centralized counseling conducted by the GGSIP University, New Delhi. Remaining 10% of the seats are reserved for Management Category. Admission to these 10% Reserved Management Category Seats shall be done by the Management of the Institute, on merit based upon the rank of NIMCET-2023 or GGSIPU CET-2023 (CET Code: 105) or CUET-2023, for MCA Programme and GGSIPU CET-2023 (CET Code: 126) or CUET-2023 for BA (JMC) Programme, as per the Instructions of the Govt. of NCT, Delhi and GGSIP University, New Delhi, from time to time. Candidates desirous of taking admission in the Institute under Management Category may directly contact the Institute, in person, with their NIMCET or GGSIPU CET or CUET Rank Card and other academic records. Please note that the students who will not appear in the NIMCET-2023 or GGSIPU CET-2023 or CUET-2023 will not be eligible, in any of the Categories; Merit Seats or Management Category seats.
