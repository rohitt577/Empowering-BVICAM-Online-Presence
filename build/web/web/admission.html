Admisions in MCA Programme

During the Academic Year 2023-2024, admission to the MCA Programme of this Institute shall be done through centralized Counselling to be conducted by GGSIP University, New Delhi, based upon the rank obtained by the students in the following tests:-

  • 1. NIT MCA Common Entrance Test (NIMCET – 2023) conducted by NIT Jamshedpur. More details regarding the NIMCET-2023 can be obtained from the NIMCET website https://www.nimcet.in/.
  • 2. GGSIPU Common Entrance Test (CET-2023; CET Code-105) conduct by GGSIP University, New Delhi. More details regarding the CET-2023 can be obtained from the GGSIP University website http://www.ipu.ac.in/.
  • 3. Common University Entrance Test (CUET-2023) conduct by National Test Agency (NTA). More details regarding the CUET-2023 can be obtained from the NTA website https://nta.ac.in/.

    • Candidates should note that while making admissions, the order of the preference of various CETs shall be maintained as NIMCET – 2023: 1st Preference; GGSIPU CET-2023: 2nd Preference and CUET-2023: 3rd Preference. This means that the 1st Preference shall be given to the NIMCET qualified candidates. If seats remain vacant, after exhausting the entire Merit List of the NIMCET Qualified candidates, GGSIPU CET-2023 qualified candidates shall be considered and given 2nd Preference. However, if seats still remain vacant, after exhausting the Merit List of the GGSIPU CET-2023 Qualified candidates, CUET-2023 qualified candidates shall be considered and given 3rd Preference.

Admissions in BA(JMC) Programme

During the Academic Year 2023-2024, admission to the BA (JMC) Programme of this Institute shall be done through centralized Counselling to be conducted by GGSIP University, New Delhi, based upon the rank obtained by the students in the following tests:-

  • 1. GGSIPU Common Entrance Test (CET-2023; CET Code-126) conduct by GGSIP University, New Delhi. More details regarding the CET-2023 can be obtained from the GGSIP University website http://www.ipu.ac.in/.
  • 2. Common University Entrance Test (CUET-2023) conduct by National Test Agency (NTA). More details regarding the CUET-2023 can be obtained from the NTA website https://nta.ac.in/.

    • Candidates should note that while making admissions, the order of the preference of various CETs shall be maintained as GGSIPU CET-2023: 1st Preference and CUET-2023: 2nd Preference. This means that the 1st Preference shall be given to the GGSIPU CET-2023 qualified candidates. If seats remain vacant, after exhausting the entire Merit List of the GGSIPU CET-2023 Qualified candidates, CUET-2023 qualified candidates shall be considered and given 2nd Preference.

    Based upon the rank obtained by the candidates in the NIMCET-2023 or GGSIPU CET-2023 or CUET-2023, GGSIP University, New Delhi, will conduct centralized counseling for allocation of seats to the desirous candidates rank-wise. After having qualified the CET, all the candidates will have to register for the centralized counseling to be conducted by the GGSIP University, New Delhi. During the centralized counselling conducted by GGSIP University, Priority Order shall be maintained, as mentioned above, programme-wise. Further details in this regard may be obtained from the website of GGSIP University http://www.ipu.ac.in. It is, therefore, advised to all the desirous students to appear for all the CETs i.e. NIMCET-2023, GGSIPU CET-2023 and CUET-2023.
