MDP: Management Development Programmes


Among our wide portfolio of offerings at BVICAM, New Delhi, we also conduct special technology-based training programmes customized according to the requirements of working professionals of different industries. The programmes are conducted with an aim to help professionals improve and enhance their working skills and train them to have maximum efficiency and be effective future leaders. At BVICAM we closely monitor the evolving needs of our economy and organize a wide range of training programmes on contemporary themes belonging to different areas of management. Each programme is carefully structured to ensure quality and offer unique learning propositions to working professionals, while equipping them with the evolving technologies, practices, perspectives and approaches.
